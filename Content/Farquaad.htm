Lord Farquaad (or simply known as just Farquaad) was the main antagonist in Shrek.He was also the main antagonist to princess Fiona and Donkey

Lord Farquaad is the comically evil short-in-stature ruler of the huge castle of Duloc..

In his pursuit of perfection, Farquaad attempted to rid his kingdom of fairy tale creatures, offering a reward for their capture and then exiling imprisoned creatures to Shrek's swamp. However, because Farquaad is not of royal stock, he cannot become a King until he marries a princess. He then decides that Princess Fiona will be the perfect wife and Queen for him, but first she must be rescued from her tower guarded by a fire-breathing dragon.
